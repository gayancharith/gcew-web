# Sri Lanka Women's Cricket Team Advances to Semi-Finals

Sri Lanka Women's cricket team has qualified for the semi-final of the Women's Asia Cup. The captain, **Chamari Athapaththu**, has scored a remarkable **180 runs** in her 3 innings, including a magnificent century against the Malaysia Women's team.

The semi-finals will be held tomorrow, **July 25, 2024**. The matches are scheduled as follows:

- **First match**: India vs Bangladesh at 2 PM Sri Lanka time
- **Second match**: Sri Lanka vs Pakistan at 7 PM Sri Lanka time

Get ready for some exciting cricket action!

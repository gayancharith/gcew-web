# Sri Lanka Women’s Team Reaches the Women’s Asia Cup 2024 Final

The Sri Lanka women's team has qualified for the final of the Women's Asia Cup 2024 after a thrilling victory against Pakistan. Chamari Athapaththu led the team with an impressive 63 runs off 48 balls, including 9 fours and 1 six, securing the win.

Sri Lanka will face India in the final of the Women's Asia Cup 2024 on July 28th, starting at 3 pm Sri Lanka time.
